Overview

Hi, I'm John Cho. I'm a young and motivated Full Stack Web Developer based in Atlanta, Georgia whose passion is to build and innovate.

Graduating from Georgia Tech with a degree in Industrial Engineering, I took my first job as an Implementation Engineer, pursuing my dreams to be in the tech industry. From working this role, I became fascinated about web applications and discovered my true passion to become a developer.

I've finally made my dream come true through Flatiron School's Full Immersive bootcamp, and I'm extremely excited for an opportunity to work with an organization or a group of talents to build and innovate technology.

Let's join forces to accomplish great things!
